Catharsis

Experiencing catharsis through music involves finding relief and easing emotional tension by immersing yourself in songs that resonate with your current feelings. This allows you to process emotions in a controlled and safe manner, leading to a sense of comfort and decreased emotional distress. When you're feeling down and listening to sad songs, it's as if the music is expressing what you're feeling, and this expression fosters a sense of connection, where the feeling of being understood through music gives us a sense of validation and comfort.

Emotional Regulation

Listening to music triggers the release of neurotransmitters like dopamine or endorphins, which are associated with positive feelings. An example of this would be when you are sad and listening to sad music, your brain releases prolactin, which helps modulate feelings of sadness and will help your brain regulate your emotional state.

Nostalgia and Memory

Certain songs can evoke memories and associations with past experiences. Not only this, but when you listen to music that you've enjoyed during similar emotional states in the past, it can trigger nostalgia. These two things have been found to generally be a mood uplifter and can help you reminisce about past positive experiences.
